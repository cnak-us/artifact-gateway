═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  ⚠️  Cert/hostname constraint — read this first
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  EXTERNAL_HOSTNAME = {{ .Values.config.externalHostname | default "(unset)" }}

  OCI clients (docker / helm / oras) refuse to talk to the gateway unless
  the hostname they connect to is in the TLS certificate's SAN list AND
  matches `config.externalHostname` exactly.

  Mismatch → opaque "x509: certificate is valid for X, not Y" errors with
  no graceful fallback. If you see that, fix EXTERNAL_HOSTNAME or the cert.
